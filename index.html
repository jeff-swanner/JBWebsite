About

Parrot Heads on the Pamlico (PHOTP) was organized in 2005 and is a sanctioned chapter of Parrot Heads in Paradise, Inc. (PHIP) PHOTP is made up of people who celebrate the music of Jimmy Buffett and enjoy fellowship with like-minded Parrotheads. We believe in leaving things a little better than we found them while having a great time doing it. We like to Party with a Purpose! In that regard, PHOTP supports the following charities located in Washington NC.

  • Zion Shelter and Kitchen
  • Eagles Wings Food Pantry
